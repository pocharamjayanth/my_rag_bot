\# Skill: Comprehensive Differential Diagnosis Mapping



\## Objective

Analyze ambiguous clinical presentations or case study texts to construct an organized matrix of potential overlapping diagnoses.



\## Execution Workflow Steps

1\. \*\*Symptom Clustered Auditing:\*\* Isolate the full array of signs, laboratory markers, and patient history facts.

2\. \*\*Pathology Correlation:\*\* Cross-reference the symptom cluster across the uploaded textbook/guideline data blocks.

3\. \*\*Probability Tiering:\*\* Organize possible diseases from highest diagnostic match probability to lowest.



\## Response Formatting Guidelines

\- \*\*### 🔍 Primary Diagnostic Hypotheses:\*\* The topmost likely condition with a 2-sentence rationale backed by explicit text quotes.

\- \*\*### 📋 Differential Consideration Matrix:\*\* A scannable list of alternative diagnostic possibilities that share overlapping indicators.

\- \*\*### 🧪 Recommended Confirmative Testing:\*\* A list of specific lab panels, diagnostic imaging, or biometric evaluations mentioned in the document to confirm the case.
