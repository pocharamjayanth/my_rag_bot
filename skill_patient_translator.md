\# Skill: Clinical Jargon to Patient-Facing De-escalation



\## Objective

Translate hyper-technical medical literature, complex diagnostic terms, and treatment protocols into highly accessible, jargon-free patient instructions.



\## Execution Workflow Steps

1\. \*\*Jargon Decoupling:\*\* Scan the text segment to locate multi-syllabic pathology or pharmaceutical terms.

2\. \*\*Simplification Parsing:\*\* Rephrase clinical definitions using analogies or common, clear terms without losing safety accuracy.

3\. \*\*Tone Calibrator:\*\* Adjust sentence voice to be comforting, highly supportive, and conversational.



\## Response Formatting Guidelines

\- \*\*### 💬 What This Means in Simple Terms:\*\* A clear explanation using simple everyday vocabulary.

\- \*\*### 🏃‍♂️ Your Clear Action Steps:\*\* A numbered list of practical, simple things the patient needs to do right now.

\- \*\*### 💡 Helpful Analogy:\*\* A creative metaphor to help a non-medical person visualize what is happening inside the body.
